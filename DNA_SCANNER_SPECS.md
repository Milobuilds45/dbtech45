# Business DNA Scanner - Complete Specifications

## Overview
Interactive business analysis tool for dbtech45.com/tools implementing Derek's 7 Layer Framework.

## Core Concept
**Location:** dbtech45.com/tools/dna-scanner  
**Purpose:** Interactive 7 Layer Framework business analysis tool with lead generation

## Technical Implementation
- **Step-by-step wizard** (7 distinct phases mapping to the 7 layers)
- **Dynamic scoring** with real-time feedback as user progresses
- **Conditional logic** - follow-up questions based on answers
- **Data visualization** - radar chart of business strengths/weaknesses  
- **PDF report generation** - professional business analysis report
- **Email capture** - gated behind report delivery (lead gen goldmine)

## 7 Layer Framework Structure

### Layer 1: Problem/Solution Fit
**Questions:**
- What specific problem does your business solve?
- How urgent is this problem for your target customers?
- What's the current cost of NOT solving this problem?
- How do customers solve this today?

**Scoring criteria:** Problem clarity, urgency, market pain level

### Layer 2: Market Opportunity 
**Questions:**
- What's the total addressable market size?
- How fast is this market growing?
- Who are the major competitors?
- What's your competitive advantage?

**Scoring criteria:** Market size, growth rate, competitive positioning

### Layer 3: Business Model
**Questions:**
- How do you make money?
- What are your unit economics?
- What's your customer acquisition cost?
- What's the customer lifetime value?

**Scoring criteria:** Revenue clarity, profitability, scalability

### Layer 4: Go-to-Market Strategy
**Questions:**
- How will you reach customers?
- What's your marketing strategy?
- What are your distribution channels?
- How will you scale customer acquisition?

**Scoring criteria:** Channel strategy, customer acquisition plan

### Layer 5: Operations & Execution
**Questions:**
- What are your key operational processes?
- Do you have the right team?
- What systems and tools do you need?
- How will you measure success?

**Scoring criteria:** Operational readiness, team capability, systems

### Layer 6: Financial Projections
**Questions:**
- What are your revenue projections?
- What funding do you need?
- When will you break even?
- What are your key financial metrics?

**Scoring criteria:** Financial realism, funding requirements, profitability timeline

### Layer 7: Risk Management
**Questions:**
- What are your biggest risks?
- How will you mitigate them?
- What's your contingency plan?
- How will you adapt to market changes?

**Scoring criteria:** Risk awareness, mitigation strategies, adaptability
